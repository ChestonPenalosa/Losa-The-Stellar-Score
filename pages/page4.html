Disguise in Crowd

You trust your instincts and decide that the best idea would be to hide within the crowd as local merchants. Sachi decides to follow along, knowing that blending in would bring in a lot less attention and risk compared to Dom's plan. Dom, however, complains about how mundane the idea is. He goes on about the fame and glory that a loud entrance would bring to your reputation, and how you're missing out on the fun and blood-pumping adrenaline it would bring, but Sachi swiftly shuts him down.

You make your way to the hangar and park your ship. You all search through your personal belongings and closets for any clothing that resembles the local merchants in the crowd. Dom quietly sneaks some bulky explosives and hides them within his attire just in case he ever needs to use them. Once out of the ship and into the crowd, you all subtly split apart. Close enough to keep track and not lose each other but separate enough to not seem like you're all in a trio to garner suspicion. Sachi takes the lead as she pulls up her map of the possible vault location.

All seems to be going to plan, as no one seems to be paying any attention to you. However, you hear Dom saying something behind you. You turn around and see him being pulled away by one of the security guards. You quickly alert Sachi and the both of you covertly follow Dom and the guard to an empty area. The guard became suspicious of Dom due to his abnormal choice and shape of attire, with him insisting he do a routine search of Dom. Dom, knowingly possessing explosives, refuses profusely whilst seemingly getting ready to run away. The guard reaches for his pulse pistol, demanding Dom to cooperate. You and Sachi swiftly sneak up behind the guard. The two of you could easily take him down, but how so? Should you just simply knock him out, as you don't want to have unnecessary blood shed with some random guard? Or do you take the hard route and assassinate him? Who knows whether he could wake up and alert further security mid heist. Not to mention, he IS working for the corrupt government, so technically he could be just as bad as them.
