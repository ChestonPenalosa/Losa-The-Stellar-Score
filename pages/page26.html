Sell the Prototype to Local Pawner

You bring up the idea of selling it to the local pawner you know, and your team agrees but suggests taking some caution, as you set forth on the trip. Once arrived, Sachi stays in the ship to protect it, as you and Dom embark.

Once you've made it to Kinney, the pawner you've often sold to before, you reveal to him the prototype. He is instantly intrigued after remembering the rumors about the power it could wield to its user and then asks how you retrieved such an item. You hesitate for a second before revealing the big heist your team was able to pull off. Kinney stands there in silence for a moment, before having a mischievous smile form on his face. You start to ask him for an offer, but he cuts you off by pulling his weapon on you. He violently demands that you either hand over the device to him in exchange for nothing or else he will contact the authorities and have you arrested. You plead for him to change his methods, but he refutes and doubles down saying he will pull the trigger. In a panic, you think about making a last second run for it.
