About the Author

Meet the Author: Cheston Penalosa

Cheston Penalosa is currently a Digital Media student at the University of Central Florida. He is currently in his sophomore year, focusing on game design.

Cheston was born on July 5, 2006 in Orlando, Florida. Throughout his years of early schooling, he spent many years taking classes in art and engineering. It wasn't until junior year of high school where he finally started taking classes on game development, exploring his game-playing hobby as a possible future career. Not only did he excel and thoroughly enjoy the class, he fully committed to the idea of having a career in game development. Post high school, he decided on attending UCF, not only due to his close proximity but also since UCF had specific programs pertaining to his game developing interests.

In his senior year of high school, Cheston created his first fully-playable videogame on the platform Flowlab. However, this interactive narrative is his first time creating a fully extensive choose-your-own-adventure style narrative with extensive choices, endings, and characters.
