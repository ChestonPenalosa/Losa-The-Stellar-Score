Find Back Door

You decide that there should be enough time to find another way in. You trust Sachi in her cyber-abilities, but you don't know if there is enough time for her to hack the door and the vault entrance itself could be boobytrapped. Dom looks down in despair as his explosive tendencies have been denied yet again. But he swiftly comes back up remembering what is inside the vault.

You and Dom search over the fortified walls, and notice nothing resembling a hidden entrance. Sachi pulls out her drone from earlier to scan the walls more thoroughly, and low and behold a portion of one of the vault walls sticks out compared to the rest. You equip your hand-held laser cutter and begin cutting into the less-fortified wall. After a few seconds, the wall is completely cut, as Dom holds up the cut portion to make sure it doesn't fall and make a sudden noise. You and Sachi squeeze through and finally feast your eyes on the supposed prototype. It's... a bit underwhelming. You didn't exactly know what to expect, but it certainly wasn't this. The tech resembles some sort of wand with one of the ends having protrusions resembling a claw shape. It has gold and silver accents swarming around it in a fluid-like manner. It has indents resembling areas to put fingers to hold said device

Without wasting too much time, you swiftly grab the device and replace it with one of your own cheap tools. You and Sachi run back out of the vault and gather with Dom. You are filled with the sensation of power by merely having the device in your pouch. But still, only half of this heist is over. With the prototype in your possession, you need to figure out a way to get out and escape. You think the fastest way out is to just hurry over back to the ship in the hangar before anyone even knows about the break in. By the time security is triggered, you'll be out without a trace. Understandably, Sachi thinks it's too risky and says that taking the empty maintenance shaft is the best way back even if it's old and a longer distance. Dom lights up and surmises that now is the time finally utilize his explosive potential to end this heist with a boom by running and gunning through.
