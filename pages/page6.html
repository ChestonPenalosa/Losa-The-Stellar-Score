Hack through Vault

You trust Sachi in her cyber abilities and let her try and hack through the locked vault. Dom looks down in despair as his explosive tendencies have been denied yet again. But he swiftly comes back up remembering what is inside the vault.

Sachi gets out one of her gadgets, attaches it onto the door, and quickly gets to work. One by one, you can hear subtle clicks inside the door. You feel antsy, constantly looking around and back at Sachi hoping she finishes up. Eventually, you hear a single loud click as the vault door slowly swings open. You open the door further as you all feast your eyes on the supposed prototype. It's... a bit underwhelming. You didn't exactly know what to expect, but it certainly wasn't this. The tech resembles some sort of wand with one of the ends having protrusions resembling a claw shape. It has gold and silver accents swarming around it in a fluid-like manner. It has indents resembling areas to put fingers to hold said device.

Suddenly, the alarms begin blaring off before you've even taken it all in. Without wasting too much time, you swiftly grab the device, and you all gather to a hidden area to quickly think up an escape plan. Sachi sends out her drone to scout the area to see how much time you guys have: unsurprisingly not much. You think the fastest way out is to just run straight to the hangar and into your ship. Surely you can get over and out before they figure out it was you. The suddenness of the alarms and panic of the crowds should buy you enough time. Dom dismisses the idea for its lack of force and suggests going for a massive run and gun through. This kind of adrenaline-pumping escape will surely get you out and stamp your names in history books.
