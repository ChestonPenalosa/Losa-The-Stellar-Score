Give up Prototype - END

In a panic, you decide to hand the prototype over to Kinney. Dom looks at you in shock, as Kinney demands the both of you to leave immediately or else he will contact authorities. You and Dom swiftly leave and head back to your ship, as you reveal what happened to Sachi. As you fly off, you feel an instant wave of regret and guilt about what happened, and that you should've known that it was a bad idea. Dom goes to say something, when suddenly you hear sirens becoming louder. You check the surroundings only to find out that regardless of what happened, Kinney called the authorities on you anyway. You continue to pilot the ship as Sachi and Dom immediately head to the ship's weaponry, eventually losing the authority on your tail. You sulk in shame, knowing that not only have you lost the one thing you risked your life for in the heist, but got nothing in exchange.

...But Dom continues what he was saying earlier. He says that instead of accepting defeat, you should go and take it right back from Kinney. After a second of thinking, you feel a sense of vigor before turning your ship right back around.
