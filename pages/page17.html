Destroy the Device - END

Sachi and Dom stand there, arguing about what exactly to do with the device. After some moments thinking to yourself, you grab each end of the prototype, and with all your might snap it in half across your knee. The device glows briefly before immediately dimming down, with some blue sparks flying out. You can hear it pulsing, like a beating heart, before falling silent. Sachi and Dom, too, fall immediately silent, shocked with your decision. But you explain to them that no one, not even your team, can be trusted with the device. If people in power were to take it from them, who knows what deeds they could do. They feel a little enraged, especially considering how much danger that heist put them in, but after some time come around and understand your conscience. In the end, you open the ship's trash chute and throw the remains out into deep space.

Months go by, and in that time a lot has changed. News has already spread around the galaxy of a heist pulled off by a group of 3 in which they stole the prototype kept in the vault, and their main suspects: you, Sachi, and Dom. The remains of the device were even found floating in deep space, but the conditions of space caused further damage, making the device irreparable and useless. They were only able to recover vague, blurry footage of your team and your ship. However, that was enough for certain people to identify you. People in power have spent their resources trying to hunt you down. Luckily, certain people in the crime world have contacted you with a deal: they offer some protection from governmental authorities in exchange for the services and skills of your team. In the end, your team has become quite popular in the underworld, completing missions, getting treasures, and making money.
