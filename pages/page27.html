Keep the Prototype, Lost Team Member to Fall - END

You realize that simply giving away or destroying the device would mean that Dom's life was meaningless in this. To break the awkward silence in the ship, you reveal to Sachi what was going on in your mind when it all happened. You hand the device over to Sachi, as she sets up her utilities to begin analyzing the prototype. However, she doesn't speak a word to you.

Months go by, and in that time a lot has changed. Sachi has eventually come around to speaking to you again but has this cold and cut off manner. You and Sachi have mourned Dom and even held a secret funeral for him. Sachi still hasn't fully understood the device or how it works but only knows that it contains elements that so far don't exist in this galaxy. News has already spread around the galaxy of a heist pulled off by a group of 3 in which they stole the prototype kept in the vault, and how Dom was one of the casualties of your team. They were only able to recover vague, blurry footage of you and Sachi fleeing in your ship. However, that was enough for certain people to identify you. You've been nearly ambushed, followed, hunted, threatened multiple times. People in power and even the government have tried contacting you for negotiation requests. But you ignore them, as you know that one day, you will be able to fully wield unimaginable power, even if you must live on a dangerous line until then.

THE END - POWERFUL BUT HUNTED #3
