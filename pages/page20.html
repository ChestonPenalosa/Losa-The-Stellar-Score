Save the Device

Tempted by the potential power you could have, you reach for the falling device, barely grabbing before it rolls off the edge. You try reaching for Dom's rope, but before you can, Dom's grip had severely loosened. You take your last look at Dom as he plummets down into the dark abyss below, his echoing screams becoming quieter, until you hear a final splash. Sachi pulls you up as you both stare at where Dom once was, as the sound of running water floods your ears.

Before you know it, you hear alarms blaring off in the distance. You and Sachi immediately sprint to the hangar, enter your ship, and start flying off. You look in the rear-view cameras and realize that some security ships have started following you. Sachi arms your ship's onboard guns and takes care of the ones remaining on your tail, as you two narrowly escape. Once the heat dies down, silence fills the ship as the once trio has now turned duo. The only thing left to do is decide the fate of the device: do you keep something this powerful to yourself, sell it, or even destroy it?
