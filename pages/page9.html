Blast Open Vault

You decide to let Dom do his thing and blast open the door. Sachi stares in disappointment, saying this is clearly not a good idea but knows there aren't many other options.

Dom uses his best explosive, one that he knows will get through the vault door. He throws it and you all back away far enough from the upcoming explosion. Dom holds the detonator and counts down from 3... 2... 1... clic-BOOM. The vault door swings wide open; your ears start to subtly ring. As the rubble and smoke clears, you all finally feast your eyes on the supposed prototype. It's... a bit underwhelming. You didn't exactly know what to expect, but it certainly wasn't this. The tech resembles some sort of wand with one of the ends having protrusions resembling a claw shape. It has gold and silver accents swarming around it in a fluid-like manner. It has indents resembling areas to put fingers to hold said device.

Without wasting too much time, you swiftly grab the device, and you all gather to a hidden area to quickly think up an escape plan. Sachi sends out her drone to scout the area to see how much time you guys have: unsurprisingly not much. You think the fastest way out is to just run straight to the hangar and into your ship. Surely you can get over and out before they figure out it was you. The suddenness of the alarms and panic of the crowds should buy you enough time. Dom dismisses the idea for its lack of force and suggests going for a massive run and gun through. This kind of adrenaline-pumping escape will surely get you out and stamp your names in history books. Besides, you've already blown up the vault, how much more damage will make a difference?
