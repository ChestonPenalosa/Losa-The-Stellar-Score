Sell the Prototype to Black Market - END

You bring up the idea of selling it to the black market, and your team agrees but suggests taking some caution. You make your way to the planet Natronin, known for its abundance of criminal activity and lack of government presence. Once arrived, Sachi stays in the ship to protect it, as you and Dom embark to black market vendors. Dom keeps his weaponry on full display to veer off any threats.

Once you've made it to Mobba, one of the most powerful and influential gangsters of the underworld, you reveal to him the prototype. He is instantly intrigued after remembering the rumors about the power it could wield to its user and asks what you want in return. You demand 50 million labloons, and he immediately agrees to a deal. Dom chimes in, doubling it to 100 million, and again Mobba agrees. Dom goes for another raise, but you instantly stop him as going any higher could risk the deal. Instead, Mobba adds another tag to the deal: exchange for your teams' skills and services to fulfill tasks and missions for him. Without hesitation, you agree, handing off the device and getting a currency card in return holding the 100 million labloons. Mobba says that he hopes to contact you again in the future. You and Dom make your way back to the ship and fly off.

Months go by, and in that time a lot has changed. News has already spread around the galaxy of a heist pulled off by a group of 3 in which they stole the prototype kept in the vault. They were only able to recover vague, blurry footage of you and Sachi fleeing in your ship. However, that was enough for certain people to identify you. You've been nearly ambushed, followed, hunted, threatened multiple times, but they've been no match for you. Mobba has given your team multiple missions, including heists, bounties, hitlists, and more. You've been making money and upgrades to your team's arsenal and ship. However, one small thing crosses your mind every now and then: what happened to the prototype? You've heard no updates or news about it, and Mobba always denies giving you information when you've asked. Your bandit life has changed, for better or worse, to a personal criminal agent, and you now fear the consequences of the deal you had way ago.

THE END - LEVELED UP BUT...?
