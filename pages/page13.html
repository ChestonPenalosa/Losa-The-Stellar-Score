Maintenance Shaft

You agree with Sachi, that navigating through the old maintenance shaft is the best idea. Trying to sneak your way back to the ship directly might be too risky. Dom yawns in boredom and simply just starts thinking about what to do with the device once you've escaped.

You all sneak out the vault area and head towards the maintenance shafts. A couple of guards and standing in front of the entrance, so Sachi sends out a fake message to their radios requesting assistance to another area. Once the guards leave, you three sneak over through the entrance. None of you really know what the maintenance shaft is like, so it takes you by surprise. The walls are old and broken down, the lights are either dimmed out or quickly flashing on and off. The halls constantly split into different halls, resembling a maze-like structure. Remembering the lack of time, you start heading towards the supposed direction to the hangar.

Seconds feel like minutes as new hallways look exactly like old ones, some even abruptly lead to dead ends, causing you to turn back. Eventually, you hear crowds walking along with the sounds of ship engines igniting. Without hesitation, all three of you start running towards the noise, hoping it leads to the hangar, with Sachi going in front and Dom falling behind. Unfortunately, the old, abandoned halls cannot handle the force, as a large portion of the structure gives way, opening a massive hole. You trip and fall over, barely hanging on the edge. Sachi turns around and tries pulling you back up. You look down, revealing the deep, dark abyss that falls beneath you, along with the echoes of running water. Dom completely fell out of the maintenance shaft and is barely holding onto a nearby robe. You almost reach over to the rope but realize how light your pouch now feels. You turn only to see the prototype has fallen out and is slowly rolling over to the edge. Sachi cannot hold onto you for much longer and emphasizes that she needs to pull you up immediately. Dom's grip on the rope is slowly loosening as he begs you to pull him up with you. The device slowly begins rolling faster and further away from your reach. You only have mere seconds to reach for the rope or reach for the device.
