Sell the Prototype

After listen to Dom rattle on, you yourself start becoming tempted by the potential luxuries and wealth the device could bring you. Ultimately the appeal becomes too great, and you agree with Dom to sell the prototype. Sachi is deeply disappointed as she was interested in examining and testing the device for its advanced tech that is foreign to this galaxy.

With its fate settled, you gaze at the prototype and its peculiar designs. If the rumors are indeed true and it holds unthinkable levels of power, then that could be grounds for unthinkable amounts of money. And if the rumors aren't true and the device is practically useless, then you're making money in return for giving away basically nothing. However, you start thinking about where exactly to sell the device. You know this one local pawner whom you've sold a couple of other stolen items, so he could possibly be trustworthy enough. The black market is also tempting, considering how much money they would be willing to offer for something of this nature. Or maybe set forth for a long journey to the nearest galaxy. They could be familiar with this kind of technology and therefore should know the power it holds, meaning more money for you.
