Knock Out Guard

You signal Sachi to only knock out the guard, and you two easily take him down. You regather with Dom and swiftly hide the body before immediately getting back on track and making it to the entrance of the vault. Fortunately, Sachi disabled the nearby security for only a short amount of time to not raise suspicion.

Now, you need a plan to get into the vault. You suggest that there could be an easier way in, possibly a back door as the main door could be boobytrapped. The disabled security could buy you enough time to find one. Sachi says that there's no guarantee there is another entrance, nor is there enough time to go and find one; it'll be easier to just hack through the vault door. Dom half agrees with Sachi but proposes another idea: blast your way through.
