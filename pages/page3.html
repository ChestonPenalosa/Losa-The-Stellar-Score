Blast Through

You agree with Dom; blasting through is the best idea. Massive explosions and alerting security invites danger, but you love the blood-pumping risk. The loud entrance will not only blow up the way in, but will blow up your reputation, getting you closer to the supposed fame and glory of being a renowned bandit. Plus, stealing the prototype from the hands of the wealthy AND blowing up their property? Absolute double punch. Sachi reluctantly follows, knowing that since both of you agree, she can't change either of your minds.

You make your way to the hangar and park your ship. Dom rapidly searches through his belongings to gather any explosive device he can carry. As you all make your way out of the ship, Dom pulls out his rocket launcher onto his shoulder, aims, and pulls the trigger. BOOM! The nearby walls crumble apart, guards knocked to ground, crowds scattering in panic, security alarms blaring off. The three of you all book it through, running as fast as can, as Dom blows up wall after wall to get to the vault.

Finally, you get to the vault. The prototype is just one blast away from your possession. Dom grabs another explosive and aims right at the door. However, you hesitate. Is this too much? Are you drawing TOO much attention? Maybe it'd be better to let the heat die down just a bit and hide with the panicked crowd. Then when least expected, jump right back into heist mode. Or, you could keep going forward and have Dom blast your way through.
