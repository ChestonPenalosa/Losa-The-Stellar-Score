Flee with Prototype - END

In a panic, you signal to Dom, as you both immediately book it out. You can see and hear Kinney's bullets fly by you as you escape. You and Dom make it back to the ship and you reveal what happened to Sachi. As you fly off, you feel an instant wave of regret and guilt about what happened, and that you should've known that it was a bad idea. Suddenly you hear sirens becoming louder. You check the surroundings only to find out that Kinney indeed called the authorities on you. You continue to pilot the ship as Sachi and Dom immediately head to the ship's weaponry, eventually losing the authority on your tail. Once the head dies down, your team agrees that you should probably keep the device. You hand the device over to Sachi, as she sets up her utilities to begin analyzing the prototype. She says that it will likely take her while before they can effectively start testing the prototype.

Months go by, and in that time a lot has changed. Sachi still hasn't fully understood the device or how it works but only knows that it contains elements that so far don't exist in this galaxy. News has already spread around the galaxy of a heist pulled off by a group of 3 in which they stole the prototype kept in the vault, and their main suspects: you, Sachi, and Dom. They were only able to recover vague, blurry footage of your team and your ship. However, that was enough for certain people to identify you. You've been nearly ambushed, followed, hunted, threatened multiple times. People in power and even the government have tried contacting you for negotiation requests. But you ignore them, as you know that one day, you will be able to fully wield unimaginable power, even if you must live on a dangerous line until then.

THE END - POWERFUL BUT HUNTED #4
