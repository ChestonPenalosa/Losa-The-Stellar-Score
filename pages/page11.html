Kill Guard

You signal Sachi to full on kill the guard. She gives a little surprised expression before swiftly slitting the guard's throat. Blood pours down his uniform as he falls to the ground, limp and lifeless. Moving past the bit of shock, you regather with Dom and swiftly hide the body before immediately getting back on track and making it to the entrance of the vault. Unbeknownst to you, the guards have a cardio-implant that detects the beat of the heart, and if that beat were to ever stop, the security alarms immediately go off. The three of you all book it through, running as fast as can.

Finally, you get to the vault. The prototype is just one door away from your possession. Dom stares at the door ready, reaching for one of his explosives. However, you hesitate. Is this too much? Are you drawing TOO much attention? Maybe it'd be better to let the heat die down just a bit and hide with the panicked crowd. Then when least expected, jump right back into heist mode. Or, you could keep going forward and have Dom blast your way through.
