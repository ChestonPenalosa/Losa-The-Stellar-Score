Sell the Prototype to Neighboring Galaxy - END

You bring up the idea of selling it to the neighboring galaxy, and your team agrees but suggests taking some caution. You set forth on a long trip to the Princior Galaxy. Once arrived, Sachi stays in the ship to protect it, as you and Dom embark to local vendors.

Once you've made it to Palves, one of the most powerful and influential vendors of Princior, you reveal to him the prototype. He is instantly intrigued after remembering the rumors about the power it could wield to its user and asks what you want in return. You demand 50 million labloons, and he immediately agrees to a deal. Dom chimes in, doubling it to 100 million, and again Palves agrees. Dom goes for another raise, but you instantly stop him as going any higher could risk the deal. You hand over the device, and Palves tells you to wait whilst he goes to retrieve your earnings. You and Dom sit there patiently, when you suddenly hear sirens coming closer. You look around, only to see that you've been surrounded by local authority. Without hesitation, you and Dom start booking it back to the ship, but they prove too much to handle. Dom goes down first, as they were able to shoot his leg and contain him. You initially stop to try and help him, but they too bundle you in handcuffs. Princior appears one last time, thanking you for retrieving this powerful prototype for him, before sending you off in chains. The fate of Dom and Sachi remain unknown to you.

THE END - SCAMMED AND IMPRISONED
