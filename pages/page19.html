Save Dom - END

You reach for the rope that Dom's barely holding onto and yank it up with all your might. Dom flies back up, as all three of you land flat onto the floor of the maintenance shaft, catching your breath. After a few seconds, you remember that the device was slowly rolling off towards the edge. You look back to where it once was and nothing remained. As you all stand up and head back to your ship, you feel a sense of defeat. The purpose of this heist was solely for that device, and you could've saved it. But realizing the fact that you saved Dom's life makes you feel some pride in yourself. After all, the prototype could've been useless for all you know. As you all enter the ship and start flying away, you hear subtle alarms blaring off behind you.

Months go by, and in that time a lot has changed. News has already spread around the galaxy of a heist pulled off by a group of 3 in which they stole the prototype kept in the vault, and their main suspects: you, Sachi, and Dom. The remains of the device were even found floating in the local sewage system, but the conditions caused further damage, making the device irreparable and useless. They were only able to recover vague, blurry footage of your team and your ship. However, that was enough for certain people to identify you. People in power have spent their resources trying to hunt you down. Luckily, certain people in the crime world have contacted you with a deal: they offer some protection from governmental authorities in exchange for the services and skills of your team. In the end, your team has become quite popular in the underworld, completing missions, getting treasures, and making money.
