To You, The Reader

For the Audience

"Losa: The Stellar Score" is an interactive narrative intended purely for entertainment purposes brought to you by Chestah's Chronicles. You, the reader, are transported to another galaxy, living the life as a renowned bandit. With a new found goal of stealing a piece of treasure, together with your own ragtag team, you are faced with various distinct choices, each putting you on a new path into multiple different endings.

If you are any at all interested in science fiction stories about space and galactic worlds or interested in narratives involving grand heists, then this story could easily appeal to you. To begin, click on the 'Home' button in the navigation bar.
