Destroy the Prototype - END

You start thinking about the power the device holds and whether you or anyone could be trusted with it. To break the awkward silence in the ship, you reveal what happened to Dom to Sachi, how he went out doing what he loved most, with a bang. Sachi asks what you plan on doing with the device, and you reply with how you want to destroy it. Sachi takes offense to this and quickly pulls out her cyber handgun and aims it at you. She lectures about how the prototype could have unthinkable power and that destroying it means that Dom's life was merely nothing to you, as she demands you hand it over. You refuse to and a battle breaks out between you. She eventually gains the upper hand, grabs the device, and launches herself out of the ship through one of the escape pods. You lay there defeated, deafened by the silence in your ship. All alone, as bullet holes scatter the walls.

Months go by, and in that time a lot has changed. You held a secret funeral for Dom and mourn the loss of your once trio. Since that day, you've heard nothing of Sachi's whereabouts or about the prototype. News has already spread around the galaxy of a heist pulled off by a group of 3 in which they stole the prototype kept in the vault, and how Dom was one of the casualties of your team. They were only able to recover vague, blurry footage of you and Sachi fleeing in your ship. However, that was enough for certain people to identify you. You've been nearly ambushed, followed, hunted, threatened multiple times. People in power and even the government have tried contacting you for negotiation requests as everyone still thinks that you possess the sacred device. You went into this heist thinking about everything you could gain, only to leave it losing everything you've had.

THE END - BETRAYED #2
