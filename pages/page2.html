Sneak through Vents

You agree with Sachi; sneaking through the vents will be your best bet. It would be a whole lot safer and simpler to not alert any attention to yourselves, minimizing risk and danger. Dom reluctantly agrees with this "boring" plan, saying that you'll be missing out of the fame and glory that a loud entrance would bring to your reputation. However, he knows the danger of said idea, so he follows Sachi for now.

You make your way to the hangar and park your ship. Sachi sends out an invisibility-cloaked drone to search around the premises for a possible entrance to the ventilation system, and after some time she finds one that is reachable and unguarded. You all exit the ship and sneak your way over to the drone, making sure to keep your faces hidden from any guards and cameras. Once there, you shoot your grapple gun to make it up to the "ventrance", but Sachi interjects, "I have the map of the ventilation system, let me go first." You let her grapple first, with you and Dom quickly following. Sachi pulls up the map on her specs, and you all make your way closer to the vault. Eventually, Sachi exits the vents, and you all end up right at the vault door. Fortunately, Sachi disabled the nearby security for only a short amount of time to not raise suspicion. Unfortunately, the vents end right at the door and do not go further into the vault.

Now, you need a plan to get into the vault. You suggest that there could be an easier way in, possibly a back door as the main door could be boobytrapped. The disabled security could buy you enough time to find one. Sachi says that there's no guarantee there is another entrance, nor is there enough time to go and find one; it'll be easier to just hack through the vault door. Dom half agrees with Sachi but proposes another idea: blast your way through.
