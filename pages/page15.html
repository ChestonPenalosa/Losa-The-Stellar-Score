Straight to Hangar - END

You think you can take advantage of the chaotic situation and try to book it back to your ship. Neither Sachi nor Dom think that this is the best idea. Dom goes to pull out his weapon, but you quickly dismiss him, saying that it will only draw unwanted attention.

You all run into the panicked crowds, trying your best to stick together. But the rapid and unpredictable movement of the people prove too much for all of you to handle, and you end up losing sight of both Sachi and Dom. You frantically try to peer over the crowds trying to see some sort of semblance of them, when you suddenly feel someone grab your wrist. You turn only to see a security guard right in front of your face. You try to wriggle out and reach for your pocket blade, but another guard immediately zaps you with his electrobaton. You feel magnetic handcuffs lock your wrists together as you are taken away from the crowds. You were so close to unimaginable power, but the hectic situation proved too much for you, as you halted right at the doorstep. The fate of Sachi and Dom remain unknown to you as the life of a renowned bandit you once knew ends in metal chains.

THE END - ARRESTED #2
