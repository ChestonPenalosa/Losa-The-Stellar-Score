Run and Gun

The temptation of glory and your name echoing throughout the galaxy compels you to agree with Dom. Sachi deeply rejects the idea and doesn't want any part of it. Dom searches through his arsenal, railgun in one hand and explosives in the other, as you equip your own pulse rifle.

You all sneak out of the vault area and into the main area, riddled with guards. Sachi goes straight forward, leaving you and Dom behind. Dom signals you to get ready, as he throws a plasma bomb towards one of the guards and presses the bu-BOOM! The crowds scatter in a chaotic panic. Nearby guards start heading in your direction, the alarms blaring off. You and Dom sprint towards the hangar, shooting any guards that you see in your path. Dom fires his railgun wildly whilst throwing more explosives. You aim your pulse rifle at any guard, pulling your trigger before they can. With escape coming close, you turn around to check on Dom, only to see the worst. Guards are swarming Dom as his arsenal runs low. Before you can react, another guard starts running at you. You immediately shoot your weapon at him, only to find you've run out of ammo. Without thinking, you reach into your pouch and equip the prototype. The device seemingly has no inputs or buttons of any kind, so you don't know how it works. Even then, you aim at the guard and riddled in fear, you merely think about blasting awa-ZWOOM! A bright, powerful laser shoots out the clawshaped end of the prototype, blasting the guard and knocking you back. With him out of the way, you try to look for Dom, only to see more guards coming in your direction. You immediately dart to the hangar and into your ship, which Sachi is already in. She asks where Dom is, but you alert her that it's too late and that you need to leave NOW. Without hesitation, you start the ship and Sachi arms the ship's guns. Some security ships follow you, but Sachi takes care of them, as you two narrowly escape. Once the heat dies down, silence fills the ship as the once trio has now turned duo. The only thing left to do is decide the fate of the device: do you keep something this powerful to yourself, sell it, or even destroy it?
